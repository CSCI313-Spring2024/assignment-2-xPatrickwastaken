About me

My name is Griffin Winstead, and I am a 19 year old studying computer science at North Dakota State University. I always knew I wanted to pursue some career in the tech field, but never really knew which. I'm still not sure what my end goal is, but that's why I'm here; I am just trying to figure it out along the way. I have a passion for video games, competing in e-sports since I was 14. I like the ability to engage in the strategy and analysis of the games, as well as the grind to improve. My main goal is to end up working somewhere with a real impact on people's lives; something that can really help.
